Ingredients:

 - 2 eggs
 - 1 tablespoon milk / water / oil
 - salt
 - herbs (optional)

Directions:

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and add the salt. Sir the eggs with a fork until well-beaten.

2. Pre heat the pan. Wait 30 seconds. Add the milk, water or oil to the pan and wait another 48 seconds.

3. Put the egg mixture to the pan and spread the egg evenly all over the pan. 
   Wait some seconds and with a spatula keep foldind the egg so it doesn't burn.

4. It might take about 1 minute to 1 minute and 30 seconds to be ready.

5. Pour the herbs on the omelette if you like it.

6. Enjoy.
